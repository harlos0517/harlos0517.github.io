    • Other:

  2. Confirmation and Deposit Payment

    After the discussion, I will send you the quote via Email. Please reply to confirm after you have paid the deposit. The deposit is half of the total commission price, or 30 USD if the total is less than 60 USD. Once the deposit is paid, it will not be refunded if you cancel the commission.

    You can choose to pay via bank transfer (Taiwan), Paypal (overseas, additional fee may apply) or donation platform like Deerdonate and Ko-fi.

  3. Draft Preview and Modification

    I will provide a draft preview of the music. You have two chances to request for modification. If you are still not satisfied, the deposit will not be refunded.

  4. Completion and Payment

    When the work is near completion, you will need to make the final payment. I will send the files via Email or Google Drive after the completion. Please reply to confirm that you have received the files. If the files are too large, they will be sent via Google Drive. Once you have confirmed receving, you cannot request for the files again, so please keep them safe.

  5. Other Usages of Work

    If you have other usages for the work (e.g. commercial use for non-commercial commission), you can purchase the license for an additional fee.
