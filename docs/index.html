委託流程

1. 請透過 Discord / Messenger / Twitter 私訊我委託細節。
  • 大名：(該如何稱呼你)
  • 音樂用途：(遊戲、微電影、角色主題曲...等)
  • 商用/買斷需求：
  • 音樂時長：
  • 想要的音樂風格：(音樂類別、或是用一些形容詞也可以)
  • 特別想要使用的樂器：
  • 參考用樂曲：(有的話幫助會非常大)
  • 混音需求：
  • 其他需求：
  • 截止日期：
2. 一起討論需求細節與可行性，並給你最後報價。
  • 為確保雙方權益，對話紀錄將進行截圖並保存。
3. 以 E-mail 確認並繳交訂金。
  • 訂金為總委託金額之一半，若未滿一千則以一千計。
  • 一旦繳付訂金後，若又反悔取消委託，將不返還訂金。
4. 先行製作草稿 (例如：只有主旋律和鋼琴伴奏) 給予試聽。
  • 限改稿兩次，滿兩次後仍不滿意得取消委託，唯訂金不予返還。
5. 進入混音階段前，會將半成品給予試聽，試聽沒問題後繳付尾款。
  • 限修改兩次，滿兩次後仍不滿意得取消委託，唯訂金不予返還。
6. 進入混音階段，完成後一律以 E-mail 寄出，確認委託完成。
  • 請務必回信表示已收到檔案。
  • 若檔案過大會以雲端硬碟傳送，確認收到後不得再次索取，請妥善保存。
7. 若事後有額外用途的需求 (例如：非商業委託需做商業使用)，可以加價購買授權。

Commission Process

1. Please contact me via Discord/Messenger/Twitter with details below.
  • Your name: (How I should call you)
  • Music Usage: (Game, Movie, Charatcer theme ... etc.)
  • Commercial/Perpetual:
  • Music length:
  • Preferred Music style: (Genres or adjectives)
  • Preferred instruments:
  • Reference Song/Piece: (would be very helpful if provided)
  • Mixing needs:
  • Other:
  • Deadline:
2. Discuss together to see if it works, and I'll give you the quoted price.
  • To ensure rights of both parties, conversations will be screenshotted and preserved.
3. Confirm via E-mail, and pay the deposit.
  • The amount of deposit is half the quoted price but at least USD$ 40.
  • Deposit will not be returned once you have paid it.
4. I'll make a draft (e.g.: Only melody and piano accompaniment) for you to listen.
  • You can request for modification up to twice. After that, if you are still not happy with it, you shall cancel the commission, though deposit will not be returned
5. Before going into mixing, I'll send you the half-finished result. If it sounds good to you, you shall pay the rest.
  • You can request for modification up to twice. After that, if you are still not happy with it, you shall cancel the commission, though deposit will not be returned.
6. Starts mixing. I will send via E-mail once completed, to confirm the commission is completed.
  • Please reply the E-mail indicating you have recieved the result.
  • I will send the result via Google Drive if the files are too large. You may not request for the files again after confirmation. Please keep the files safely.
7. If you wish to use the commission result in other ways (e.g.: Commercial use for commission that was non-commercial), you can always ask and pay extra afterwards.
